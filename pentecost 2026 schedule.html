Food Safety — Non-Negotiable
🌡 Hot food: 135°F minimum at all times. No exceptions.
🧊 Cold food: 40°F or below at all times.
⛔ Nothing sits at room temp. Ever.
⛔ Chafers load at 1:30PM only. Not before.
🍝 Noodles + sauce travel SEPARATE. Mix at venue only.
🍗 Chicken arrives fully cooked, diced, THAWED. Never frozen.
🍟 Plantains on turkey fryers only. Not on grill.
🧤 Large nitrile gloves only. No Sam's prep gloves.
🏷 Label every pan: school name + contents.
🎨 Color tape all equipment by school. Inventory in/out.
